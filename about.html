About Me

Hi! I'm Yaya Harman. I'm originally from the Bay Area, CA but now call Aspen, CO home. I am a student at the University of Michigan majoring in Information Analysis with a minor in Statistics.

I am an analytics intern and the Head Student Manager for Michigan Hockey. From sharpening skates to building complex data visualizations for the coaches, there isn't an ask too big or small. My attention to detail, problem-solving, and willingness to learn have flourished and developed in this role.

I aspire to stay in college or junior hockey in an equipment, operations, or analytics role. I am always looking for more experience in these areas to expand my knowledge and understanding of the industry.
